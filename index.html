THE JUSTICE
PROTOCOLE

The blockchain ushers a world of contracts enforced by code.

Smart contracts are smart enough to self-execute as programmed, but not to evaluate complex information or to render subjective judgments.

Human involvement is necessary when a dispute arises, when circumstances have drastically changed since the moment the contract was signed and when a high stakes decision must be made.

Kleroterion is a decision making protocol which uses the blockchain, crowdsourcing and game theory to settle disputes and to modify or revoke smart contracts in a fast, transparent and inexpensive way.

An entrepreneur hires a freelancer for a software development gig. A dispute arises about the scope of the contract.

Should the freelancer give the money back to his client?

A startup raises money with a crowdfunding campaign. Funds will be released after certain milestones are reached. Should the next payment be made?

A band uploads a song on a music platform. The song is flagged for plagiarism. Should the song be deleted?

HOW IT WORKS?

Contract

Users create a smart contract and select Kleroterion as arbitration protocol

Dispute

A dispute arises, and the relevant information is sent to Kleroterion

Jury

A jury is drawn from the crowd. Jurors evaluate evidence and vote a decision.

Decision

The decision is enforced by the smart contract.

APPLICATIONS

Kleroterion is a protocol for fast, inexpensive, honest and transparent justice.

An entrepreneur hires a freelancer for a software development gig.
